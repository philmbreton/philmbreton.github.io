Hiking

NH48

I am working on hiking all 48 4000-foot mountains in New Hampshire. I have been working on this for almost six years, and plan to end this Summer 2026. I find hiking in the White Mountains incredibly rewarding. The challenge has helped me grow and push my limits, and I have gotten to spend countless hours along the way taking in the beauty of nature.

Currently, I have hiked 39 out of the 48 mountains. Here is a list of the mountains that I have left and their elevations:

  • Carter Dome (4832')
  • Middle Carter (4610')
  • Garfield (4500')
  • South Carter (4430')
  • Wildcat A (4422')
  • Zealand (4260')
  • Wildcat D (4050')
  • Moriah (4049')
  • Owl's Head (4025')

I plan to add pictures to this section.
