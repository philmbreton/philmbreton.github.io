The History of Mathematics

By: David Burton

I am currently reading The History of Mathematics by David Burton as part of a Site-Based Lincensing Plan to become a licensed mathematics teacher in New Hampshire. This book will help me acquire the knowledge for competency 10 outlined in New Hampshire Administrative Code Section 507.25.

I chose this book for the topic because I enjoyed Burton's book Elementary Number Theory when I read it for a course at the University of New Hampshire.
